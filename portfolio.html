Gates of Olympus Slot

Gates of Olympus is a slot game developed by Pragmatic Play, built around ancient Greek mythology. The game places players among the clouds of Mount Olympus, where Zeus presides over a backdrop of golden columns and celestial light. The visual design draws on classical imagery — marble pillars, glowing orbs, and richly coloured gemstone symbols populate the reels against a deep blue sky. The soundtrack reinforces the mythological atmosphere with orchestral tones that build during bonus sequences. The overall aesthetic presents a polished, high-production environment that has contributed to the game's enduring popularity across regulated online markets.

How do you play Gates of Olympus?

Gates of Olympus operates on a six-reel, five-row grid using a cascading reel mechanic rather than traditional paylines. The game uses a cluster pay system, meaning wins are awarded when eight or more matching symbols land anywhere on the grid simultaneously. After a winning cluster lands, the contributing symbols disappear and new ones fall into their positions, potentially creating additional wins from a single spin.

The game carries high volatility, meaning wins tend to arrive less frequently but with greater magnitude when they occur. The published maximum win potential is 5,000 times the stake. The default return to player rate is 96.50%, though this varies across operator configurations.

What is the bonus round in Gates of Olympus?

The Free Spins bonus is triggered by landing four or more scatter symbols — depicted as golden hourglasses — anywhere on the grid during a single spin or cascade sequence. Four scatters award 15 free spins, five award 20, and six or more award 25.

During free spins, Zeus appears at the start of each spin and randomly strikes symbols on the reels, replacing them with multiplier values ranging from 2x to 500x. These multipliers accumulate — added together rather than applied individually — and the combined total is applied to any winning cluster formed during that spin. The round can be retriggered by landing three or more additional scatter symbols, adding ten spins each time.

What extra features does Gates of Olympus have?

Gates of Olympus includes a tumble mechanic that operates throughout both base game and bonus play. Every time a winning cluster forms, the contributing symbols are removed and replaced by new ones falling from above. This process repeats until no new winning combinations form, allowing multiple wins from a single paid spin.

An Ante Bet option increases the cost of each spin by 25% in exchange for a higher probability of triggering the scatter symbols. A Buy Feature option is available in jurisdictions where permitted, allowing direct access to the free spins round at a set multiple of the current stake.

Where can I play Gates of Olympus?

Gates of Olympus is available directly on this page, compatible with desktop computers, tablets, and mobile devices. Built using HTML5 technology, the game runs through a web browser without additional software downloads. A stable internet connection is recommended, particularly during bonus sequences where the cascading mechanic generates extended animation sequences.

What are the different symbols in Gates of Olympus?

The scatter symbol is the golden hourglass. Landing four or more anywhere on the grid triggers the free spins bonus. There is no traditional wild symbol — the multiplier orbs summoned by Zeus during free spins serve as the primary value-altering mechanic. The symbol set divides into lower-paying gemstones in four colours and higher-paying divine objects: a ring, an hourglass, a goblet, and a crown.

Starburst Slot

Starburst is a slot game developed by NetEnt, set against a deep space backdrop filled with luminescent gemstones and cosmic light effects. The reels display jewel-shaped symbols in rich colours — purple, orange, yellow, blue, and green — that pulse and shimmer against a dark galactic background. How do you play Starburst?

Starburst uses a five-reel, three-row layout with ten paylines. Unlike most slots, Starburst pays both ways — winning combinations are evaluated from left to right and from right to left simultaneously, effectively doubling the ways to form a winning combination on each spin.

The game operates at low volatility, designed to deliver frequent smaller wins. The published maximum win potential is 500 times the stake. The default return to player rate is 96.09%.

What is the bonus round in Starburst?

Starburst does not feature a traditional free spins bonus triggered by scatter symbols. The primary bonus mechanic is the Starburst Wild re-spin feature, which activates when a Starburst Wild symbol lands on reels two, three, or four.

When a Starburst Wild appears, it expands to cover the entire reel and locks in position, triggering a re-spin. If an additional Starburst Wild lands on a different eligible reel during the re-spin, it also expands and locks, triggering another re-spin. This process continues until no new Starburst Wilds appear or all three eligible reels are covered, allowing up to three consecutive re-spins per activation.

What extra features does Starburst have?

Beyond the Starburst Wild re-spin mechanic, the game's both-ways pay structure functions as a persistent feature on every spin, increasing the frequency of winning combinations relative to single-direction payline slots. This mechanic contributes directly to Starburst's low-volatility profile.

Starburst does not include a gamble feature, buy feature, or progressive jackpot mechanic. The game's design philosophy prioritises accessibility and consistency over complex bonus structures, which has contributed to its longevity as an introductory title across regulated markets.
